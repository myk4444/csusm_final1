About Me

Hello. I’m Miyuki. I was born and raised in Japan. I grew up in a beautiful tropical island call Ishigaki Island. It's known for beaches and sites for snorkeling, diving and surfing. When I was young, I loved going to beaches with my parents and have picnic. My mother’s cooking was the best! My father taught me how to swim. I also taught little children swimming at Japanese swimming school.


I moved to Okinawa Island when I was 18 and moved to states when I was 26. My ex-husband, son, cat and I moved to Texas and then moved to other states from east coast to west coast. I hate driving but I drove from Virginia to California… We had an awesome cat named DeeDee, moving with a cat was very interesting. We don’t have a dog any more but we had a dog named Zeus. He was like human and I miss him so much… On my free time, I enjoy making jewelries and walk by the beach. I like Mission Bay park. In summer time, I like rent kayak and beach bike there. I also like reading tarot cards and meditate.


How I end up in Web Development… I am an Esthetician and my job gets pretty physical. It will be sad to leave esthetic field because that’s what did for last 15 years and keeps me young…

Mala Beads - How to Use it for Meditation

Buddhist prayer beads or malas are a traditional tool used to count the number of times a mantra is recited, breaths while meditating, counting prostrations.


The repetitions of a buddha’s name.
